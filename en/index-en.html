Welcome to Tarraco Veterinària

Since 2000, in Tarraco Veterinària we take care of dogs and cats in order to improve their health and well-being. We are a team of two veterinarians and a technician working with professionality and passion to give wellness and health to animals and to our clients.

Our center features modern facilities equipped with op-room, hospitalization zone, three examination rooms, lab and advanced technology for image diagnose. We also have a shop were you will find the best options for your animal nutrition.
